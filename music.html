Travel In DreamLand

That one was inspired by ABSRDST and other kawaii ~ colourbass ~ future bass artists. I was obssessed by that genre.
I remember sending a dm to Pandrezz to ask him his thoughts on my track. He replied with a collab proposal. I was so proud! I ended up turning it down, I was way too shy and insecure back then. I kinda feel dumb about it now, but hey that's life :)

FIXME

Game's Growl

I wanted to explore something mixing dubstep bass and video game synths. I really loved working on the music video. It was REALLY hard because I didn't know anything about video editing. I couldn't afford a pro software and didn't have the patience to learn, so I ended up animating it frame by frame with PNGs on a cheap video software. Took me ages, but I'm proud of the result!

FIXME

Steins;Gate - Opening Fanmade

I made this track without any specific thought at first. But when it was almost finished, I realized it was almost exactly timed to fit the Steins;Gate opening. I really loved that anime, so I arranged it into a fanmade opening video. The dubstep parts did not age well, but I still like how it turned out.
